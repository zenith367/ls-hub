ABOUT US

LESOTHO HUB

“WE CONNECT FARMERS AND BUYERS ACROSS LESOTHO TO PROMOTE SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE, FAIR TRADE, AND COMMUNITY GROWTH.” Lesotho Hub is a digital platform built to empower farmers by giving them a space to showcase their products—from crops and livestock to organics and grains.”

“We started this initiative to bridge the gap between local farmers and customers, ensuring fair pricing, transparency, and equal opportunities for all.” “Through innovation, knowledge sharing, and market access, we aim to make agriculture in Lesotho more sustainable and rewarding for everyone.”

Meet The Founder

“I’m Rorisang Thakholi, the founder and CEO of Lesotho Hub. I started this platform with a vision to empower farmers and entrepreneurs, creating a space where communities can connect and new opportunities can grow. I believe growth comes from sharing knowledge, supporting one another, and daring to dream bigger. Through Lesotho Hub, I aim to build a bridge where innovation meets tradition, and every small step forward shapes a stronger future.
